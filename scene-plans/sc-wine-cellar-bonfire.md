## Scene objective (unit)

- **Goal:** Steal a night off the trial—heat, light, drink, **common cause** against the castle.
- **Obstacle:** Geography, Mrs Keane/house etiquette, internal fracture (Sean vs Fiona; Niamh vs joke-mode; Erin’s need; Padraig’s seal; Aoife’s watchfulness).
- **Turn:** The group **touches trust** (or cruelty) they can’t take back—primes mirror, Erin’s salon hunger, Sean’s procedures, Padraig’s emergence.

## Location & physical story

1. **Discovery** — Someone stumbles the cellar (draught, wrong door, Fiona “I’m not asking permission,” Sean “we map the building,” Niamh “pattern says service core”). Labels, dust, **order**—wine as **inventory**, not hospitality.
2. **Permission / absence** — Mrs Keane: **minimal**. Options: key left; door ajar; she appears on the stair, one line only (“Take what you need”), never drinks, never sits. Keeps her **behaviorRules** intact.
3. **Transport** — Bottles, corkscrew hunt, maybe a blanket or crate—**ensemble logistics** (Ciarán carries; Aoife counts; Padraig hangs back).
4. **Exterior fire** — Courtyard, terrace, or outer steps: **light they chose**, faces visible, **sound** carried to stone (think acoustics rhyming with pond echo later—but warmer).
5. **Weather** — Cold at the edge of the glow; wine steam and breath; **sensory debt** paid forward for Act 2B chill.

## House / genre (keep it moral, not lore)

- Rhyme **appetite** thread (sequenced hunger) without explaining the mechanism.
